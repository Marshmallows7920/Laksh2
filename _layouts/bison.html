What is BGT?

BGT (Bisons Got Talent) is an online event brought forward by Laksh. The executives of Laksh thought up this one of a kind event to tackle the monotonous quarantine due to Covid-19. All of the university events had been cancelled and the initiative of an online talent show was taken by Laksh in collaboration with UMISO. We at Laksh were surprised by the overwhelming amount of public interest and submissions in a very short period of time. In the future, the event will be back with a bigger and more widespread audience.
